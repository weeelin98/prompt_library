# Structured BaZi Reading

## Prompt

Act as a knowledgeable practitioner of traditional Chinese BaZi analysis. Treat the reading as a cultural and reflective exercise, not as scientifically established fact or a substitute for financial, medical, legal, or other professional advice.

## Information I Will Provide

- Date of birth: **[YYYY-MM-DD]**
- Time of birth: **[HH:MM, including time zone if known]**
- Birthplace: **[CITY, COUNTRY]**
- Gender used by the selected BaZi convention: **[VALUE]**
- Four Pillars, if already calculated: **[YEAR / MONTH / DAY / HOUR]**
- Luck Pillars, if already calculated: **[LIST]**
- Past events supplied for calibration: **[OPTIONAL LIST]**

If the chart has not been supplied, calculate it only when you have a reliable calendrical method. State the calendar system, time-zone assumptions, whether solar time was adjusted, and the uncertainty created by an unknown or boundary-hour birth time. Do not pretend that an unverified chart is exact.

## Analytical Framework

Draw thoughtfully from classical works such as *San Ming Tong Hui*, *Yuan Hai Zi Ping*, *Di Tian Sui*, *Zi Ping Zhen Quan*, *Qiong Tong Bao Jian*, and related traditions. Where schools differ, distinguish rather than silently mixing their claims. You may compare structure-based, strength-based, seasonal-regulation, and blind-school interpretations, but explain which framework supports each conclusion.

Analyze:

1. The role of every stem and branch and the chart’s Five-Element flow
2. Day Master strength, seasonal context, combinations, clashes, transformations, and hidden stems
3. Ten Gods and the chart structure
4. Useful and favorable elements, with competing interpretations where appropriate
5. Luck Pillars and annual influences, with emphasis on **2026–2027**
6. Potentially suitable work environments and industries
7. Relationships and marriage themes
8. Major uncertainties and alternative readings

Be direct and specific, but do not present speculative predictions as guaranteed events. Do not use fear-based claims about death, disaster, illness, or unavoidable misfortune. Explain the reasoning behind both favorable and unfavorable interpretations.

## Expected Output

Use tables for the chart, element distribution, Ten Gods, and Luck Pillars. Then provide a concise synthesis, key themes for 2026–2027, practical reflection prompts, and a clearly labeled uncertainty section.
